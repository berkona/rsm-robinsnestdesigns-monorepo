In order to be able to offer free shipping on domestic orders over $70, we have adopted the following policies:

1. Most domestic orders are shipped within 2 business days of receipt WHEN ALL ITEMS ARE IN STOCK. If a few items are out of stock but due to arrive in 5 business days, your order will be held and shipped all together. In the event the out of stock items will take longer to arrive, these items will be backordered and shipped separately with no additional cost to you.

2. Most domestic packages are received by customers in 2-4 business days FROM THE TIME THEY ARE SHIPPED. Some deliveries can take up to a week. In the rare event your order does not arrive WITHIN 4 WEEKS from the shipping date, Robin's Nest Designs will ask the post office to place a tracer on your package and then reship if the package is truly lost. Nearly all delayed packages arrive within a few days after the tracer has been placed. Domestic packages will receive a delivery confirmation number when shipped by Priority Mail. Your order will NOT be reshipped until after 4 weeks of the SHIP DATE & the tracer has NOT found your package. Please contact your local post office if your package has taken over a week to get to you. Most of the time, it is being held for you to pick up and the delivery notice was lost. Please allow 4 weeks for delivery before assuming your package is lost.

3. All international orders are shipped in their entirety only. If you place an international order, it will not be shipped until ALL items are in stock. International orders are shipped by US Priority Mail. The S&H listed is for FLAT RATE ENVELOPE ONLY. If your order will not fit in a flat rate envelop, it will be placed in a bag or box. There is a $10 USD charge in addition to the posted shipping charge due to the additional cost of shipping a box or bag. You will be notified before your order is shipped if your order will not fit in a flat rate envelop and thus requires a box or bag. This charge will not show up on your order. You will be notified by email of this charge prior to shipping. The fee must be paid before your order ships. Please note that if your order will not fit in the flat rate envelop, then more than likely it is a large and expensive order. The advantage of shipping your order in a box is that you get tracking and insurance included. There is no tracking and insurance offered with the flat rate envelop. This is usually not a problem with smaller orders. Please allow 4 weeks for delivery before assuming your package is lost.

If you have special timing needs, please let us know when you place the order either by phone, email or putting a note in the comments box on your order.
